Yaghmosh

Description

Yaghmosh is a type of traditional Saudi dessert made from dates and ghee (clarified butter).
It's often served during special occasions and gatherings, and its texture is similar to a sticky toffee pudding

Ingredients

  • Lamp Meat
  • Ghee (clarified butter)

How to make it

  1. Pit and chop dates into small pieces

  2. Heat ghee (clarified butter) in a skillet over low heat until melted

  3. Add the chopped dates to the skillet and cook gently, stirring frequently, until the dates are softened and caramelized

  4. Optionally, add a pinch of ground cardamom or cinnamon for extra flavor

  5. Serve warm as a dessert, either on its own or with a drizzle of honey or a sprinkle of sesame seeds
