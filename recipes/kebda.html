Kebda

Description

Kebda refers to grilled or saut√©ed liver.
It's often seasoned with spices like cumin, coriander, and garlic, and can be served as a standalone dish or in sandwiches

Ingredients

  • Liver (typically lamb or chicken)
  • Spices (like cumin, coriander, garlic)

How to make it

  1. Clean the liver (lamb or chicken) and cut it into small pieces

  2. Marinate the liver with spices such as cumin, coriander, salt, pepper, and minced garlic

  3. Optionally, add chopped onions and parsley to the marinade for extra flavor

  4. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat and cook the liver pieces until they are cooked through and slightly browned

  5. Serve hot, either as a standalone dish or in sandwiches with bread and tahini sauce
