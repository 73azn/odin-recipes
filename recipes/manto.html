Manto

Description

Manto are stuffed dumplings, typically filled with spiced meat (often lamb or beef) and onions.
They are usually steamed and served with yogurt and sometimes a tomato-based sauce

Ingredients

  • Dumpling dough (flour, water)
  • Meat filling (spiced lamb or beef, onions)

How to make it

  1. Prepare the dumpling dough by mixing flour, water, and salt until a smooth dough forms. Let it rest for about 30 minutes

  2. For the filling, combine spiced minced meat (usually lamb or beef) with finely chopped onions, garlic, salt, black pepper, and optionally some chopped cilantro or parsley

  3. Roll out the dough into thin circles and place a spoonful of the meat mixture in the center of each circle

  4. Fold the dough over the filling to form half-moon shapes, sealing the edges tightly

  5. Steam the dumplings in a steamer basket for about 20-25 minutes, until the dough is cooked and the filling is hot

  6. Serve hot with yogurt and a tomato-based sauce
