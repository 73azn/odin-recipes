Saleeg

Description

Salleq is a traditional Saudi dish made from white rice, fried Chicken, and a blend of spices.
The fish is typically marinated and then either grilled or fried

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Chicken

How to make it

  1. Clean and rinse the rice (usually white rice) thoroughly until the water runs clear

  2. Marinate the fish (such as grouper or hammour) with spices like turmeric, cumin, coriander, salt, and minced garlic

  3. In a large pot or skillet, layer the marinated fish at the bottom

  4. Spread the rinsed rice evenly over the chicken

  5. Add water (usually in a ratio of 2:1 rice to water), cover the pot, and cook over low heat until the rice is tender and the chicken is cooked through

  6. Serve hot, garnished with fried onions and fresh herbs
