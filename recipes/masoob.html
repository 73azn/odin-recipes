Masoob

Description

Masoob is a sweet dish made from mashed bananas mixed with bread (or occasionally rice), cream, and honey.
It's often garnished with nuts like almonds or pistachios and can be enjoyed as a dessert or a snack

Ingredients

  • Bananas (mashed)
  • Bread (or occasionally rice)

How to make it

  1. Mash ripe bananas until smooth and creamy

  2. Mix the mashed bananas with pieces of torn bread (or sometimes rice) and a generous amount of cream (or milk)

  3. Add honey or sugar to sweeten according to taste, and mix well

  4. Optionally, add chopped nuts like almonds or pistachios for extra texture and flavor

  5. Serve chilled or at room temperature, garnished with more nuts if desired
